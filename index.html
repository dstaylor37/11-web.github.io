    Welcome. The following dashboards will compare various weather visualizations, examine datasets and provide to source data. To view the visualizations click on the accompanying text.

    Visualization #1: City Latitude vs. Max Temperature (Jan 5, 2017)

    A scatterplot showing the relationship between temperature and the latitude of selected cities. The nearer to 0 degrees Latitude a city is, the higher the reported max temperature.

    Visualization #2: City Latitude vs. Humidity (Jan 5, 2017)

    A scatter plot showing the relationship between humidity levels and the latitude of selected cities. We see clusters of moderately high humidity levels between 40 and 60 degrees Latitude.


    Visualization #3: City latitude vs Cloudiness (Jan 5, 2017)

    The relationship between cloudiness and the latitude of a city is shown in Figure 3. Generally speaking, the amount of cloud cover varies without much relationship to latitude.


    Visualization #4: City Latitude vs Wind Speed (Jan 5, 2017)

    The fourth and final visualzation shows the relationship between the wind speed of a city and its latitude.
